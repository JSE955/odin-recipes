Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken is my favorite food. It's crispy and delicious.

Here is an excellent recipe for it.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Oregano
  • 1 1/2 Cups Flour
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat.
  2. Season chicken to taste.
  3. Roll chicken in flour to coat.
  4. Place chicken in skillet and fry on medium heat.
  5. Turn chicken as necessary.
  6. Fry under chicken is no longer pink inside and juices run clear.
  7. Drain on paper towel and serve!

Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and Cheese will put you to sleep if it's made right.

With the right amount of cheese and butter, you'll be knocked out for hours.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. Macaroni Noodles
  • 4-5 bags of shredded cheese that you like
  • Two Sticks of Salted Butter
  • 2 cans evaporated milk
  • 2 Eggs, beaten

Steps

  1. Boil your macaroni in a big pot
  2. Drain the water from the pot
  3. Stir butter into noodles
  4. Stir cheese into noodles
  5. Stir evaporated milk into noodles
  6. Stir eggs into noodles
  7. Pour pot of noodles into a baking pan
  8. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes to an hour

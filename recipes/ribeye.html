Ribeye Steak

Description

Steak is very easy to overcook. Fortunately, it's also very easy to make juicy and delicious.

Whether you're cooking for a romantic date or you just need to wet your whistle with some gristle, this ribeye recipe will get you exactly what you need.

Ingredients

  • 1 Ribeye Steak
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Let your steak rest unrefrigerated for 15 to 20 minutes
  2. Generously add salt and pepper to both sides of your steak
  3. On a hot grill, lay steaks horizontally on one side. Grill for 90 seconds
  4. Turn steak vertically and grill for 90 seconds
  5. Flip steak to other side and grill for 90 seconds
  6. Turn steak horizonally and grill for 90 seconds
  7. Remove steak from heat, lightly butter the top and let it rest for 5 - 10 minutes

Green Beans

Description

I love green beans, fresh out the can.

Del Monte is the right way to do it.

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of Del Monte Green Beans
  • 16 oz. pack of bacon

Steps

  1. Fry your bacon in a pan
  2. Put the bacon aside and drain the bacon grease into a pot
  3. Boil the green beans with the bacon grease

Grilled Chicken Thighs

Description

Everybody loves chicken. But not everybody knows how to prepare chicken.

Follow these steps to grill some boneless, skinless thighs that'll make you want to powerbomb your mama.

Ingredients

  • 6 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Thighs
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Pepper
  • Texas Pete
  • Any other seasonings you like

Steps

  1. Wash your chicken thighs and pat them dry
  2. In a bowl, combine chicken with all seasonings above
  3. Drizzle with olive oil
  4. Mix chicken with seasonings and let marinate for 20-30 minutes
  5. Heat your grill to medium-high heat
  6. Grill chicken for 6 to 8 minutes
  7. Flip and grill for another 6 to 8 minutes or until 165 degrees internal temp
